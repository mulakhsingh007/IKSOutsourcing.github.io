Services

We offer full-cycle billing support tailored to your specialty and practice needs.

Charge Entry

Accurate charge posting with validation to reduce re-submissions and speed up reimbursements. Our team validates CPT/ICD pairing and modifiers per payer rules.

AR Follow-Up

Systematic AR follow-up to reduce outstanding balances. We track denials, pending appeals, and payer commitments until closure.

Eligibility & Benefits

Real-time eligibility verification to ensure correct patient responsibility and avoid claim denials. We document benefits and verify prior authorization requirements.

Denial Management

Root-cause analysis of denials, tailored workflows to rework and resubmit claims, and provider documentation guidance to prevent repeat denials.
